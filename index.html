About Me

My name is Hunter Leiser. I am currently studying Computer Engineering at UT Dallas and am learning full stack web development through the SMU Boot Camp program. My goal is to become competent in all respects of web dev while discovering which areas are strengths or weaknesses as well as discovering which parts I enjoy more or less. Outside of coding I enjoy creating art (mostly mixed media and drawing), playing both electric and flamenco guitar, and taking apart/rebuilding audio electronics, speakers and turntables. I would also like to combine my knowledge of software and hardware as learn more about each along the way, and possibly find creative ways to incorporate my favorite hobbies.
